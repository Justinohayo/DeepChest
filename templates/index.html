Welcome to DeepChest!

DeepChest is an AI-powered web application that helps healthcare providers analyze chest X-rays quickly and accurately. By combining advanced deep learning with intuitive design, DeepChest detects conditions like pneumonia, tuberculosis, and COVID-19 while generating heatmaps to highlight areas of concern. Designed for clinics, doctors, and patients, the platform enables secure uploads, instant AI-assisted predictions, and report generation with role-based access for data privacy.
